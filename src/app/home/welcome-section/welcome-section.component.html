Who are we?

The Federation of Kuwaiti Engineering Offices and Consulting Houses was established on Sunday 23/4/2006 AD by virtue of Law No. 38 of 1964, an organized body under the name (Federation of Kuwaiti Engineering Offices and Consulting Houses) located in Kuwait City

Why us?

The Federation of Kuwaiti Engineering Offices and Consulting Houses seeks to take care of the interests of members, defend their rights, represent engineering offices and advisory houses in front of official bodies, companies and Arab and foreign bodies, and propose rules and regulations that lead to raising the level of the engineering profession, engineering competitions, contributions and review of standard specifications in cooperation with the competent bodies
